Cancellation & Refunds Policy

Effective Date: 17 August 2025

This Cancellation & Refunds Policy explains how subscription cancellations and refund requests are handled for GENBOOK.AI services at genbookai.tech.

1. Subscription Cancellation

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time from the Billing section of your account or by contacting our support team.
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. You will retain full access until that date.
  • After cancellation, your account may be downgraded to the Free plan. Certain features and data retention may be limited.

2. Refunds

  • As a digital SaaS service, we generally do not issue refunds for partial billing periods once a charge has been processed.
  • Refund requests may be considered, at our sole discretion, in cases of:
    • Duplicate charges,
    • Proven billing errors, or
    • Where required by applicable law.
  • Trials and promotional credits, if offered, are non-refundable and not redeemable for cash.

3. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund:

  • Contact us within 14 days of the charge.
  • Provide:
    • Your account email,
    • Invoice ID, and
    • A brief description of the issue.
